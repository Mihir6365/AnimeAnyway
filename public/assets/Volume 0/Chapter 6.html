Chapter - 6

Despair and Survival

Intro

Tokyo was unusually covered in a great amount of snow.

Today, at the end of January, I was in the Sazagawa ryokan.

The garden I could see from the window in the corridor was lit up and the night’s snowy scenery spread before my eyes.

Kamogawa and I leisurely headed towards the meeting place.

On the way, Kamogawa stopped in his tracks and stared at the snowy scenery.

“Do you remember? When we were waiting for Naoe-sensei under the cold sky more than 10 years ago.”

“Yeah, like it was just the other day.”

“That day, you were put in charge of the White Room project, and I was also appointed. There were a lot of difficulties, but we were able to get this far.”

That is very true.

I have more than a couple stories that I will take to the grave without telling anyone.

“You’ve grown a lot too. I think you’ve come to understand how politics works.”

“Thank you. I was able to progress a lot working under Naoe-sense and you, Ayanokoji-san… no, Ayanokoji-sensei. My only regret is that I can’t tell my father, who passed away last year, about all that we’ve done, but…”

Around this time last year, Kamogawa’s father collapsed and died because of a heart attack.

Kamogawa wanted to directly let him know about the announcement of the White Room.

That the country is arranging an organization to take in and raise children.

Right now, the Advanced Nurturing High School for high school students is already running ahead of us, but we will go above that.

An organization to save the lives of the children who are born.

An organization to educate and turn the children into geniuses.

The White Room plan is something that will be an inevitable necessity for the world of the future.

Lives being thrown into the toilet. Lives being ended by abortion. Lives being killed by negligence.

Under the government’s guidance, this will all disappear.

This plan is also a measure against the declining birth rates.

“We will put out such results that they will reach the heavens. Don’t be satisfied by today, Kamogawa.”

“Y-yes!”

This is a special day.

Different from back then, when we were waiting under the cold sky for Naoe-sensei.

After many twists and turns, the White Room experiment has started showing real results.

Today, finally, I will give a detailed report to Naoe-sensei, and go out onto the front stage.

My steps towards the sun begins now.

Without blood, hard work, and courage, I would not have succeeded.

We sat down on our seats first, and began waiting for Naoe-sensei who is to appear on the seat of honor.

I know it would be more mannerful to wait outside, but this is an instruction from Naoe-sensei.

In other words, I understand that he also intends to show me his gratitude with this.

“After announcing this project, Naoe-sensei will finally rise up to the summit of this country.”

“Prime minister… right?”

With this, preparations for the coming election are perfectly complete.

“Not a simple prime minister. He won’t just have the honor, but also more actual power than any in history.”

In the true sense, I can say that he will be the man at the top of this country.

I don’t normally get nervous, but I can tell today that my heart is beating a little faster.

Because I’ve gambled my political life on this project.

This day, when I will be rewarded for it all… I saw it in my dreams many, many times.

“Naoe-sensei has arrived.”

After a long seeming but short 30 minutes, the notice of Naoe-sensei’s arrival reached us.

“It was faster than I thought.”

Only 10 minutes later than the agreed time.

After intending to wait for one, or two more hours without minding, I was surprised.

Now, I will seal my soft emotions, and begin a stern conversation with Naoe-sensei.

Before the curtain opened, we lowered our heads, almost touching the ground.

I started hearing Naoe-sensei’s confident and silent footsteps.

“Sorry I kept you waiting.”

After his arrival, Naoe-sensei apologized for being late.

I could not help but feel something strange towards these words.

“No, not at all. Thank you very much for taking the trouble to visit us in this cold today.”

After saying so, I cleared away the unnecessary thoughts from my mind.

There is nothing to worry about.

There is no mistaking it, I will climb up and fulfill my ambitions.

“Raise  your heads first. I can’t talk like this.”

“Yes.”

Kamogawa and I raised our heads, and I reached out to a glass to pour beer for Naoe-sensei.

However, Naoe-sensei stopped me.

“We should talk before that.”

“I apologize.”

I quickly pulled away, and began listening to Sensei.

“There is a lot to talk about, but… First, let’s start with that.”

After a slight hesitation, Naoe-sensei muttered so as if he remembered something he had forgotten.

“It’s the election for the next prime minister. I decided to pass on my candidacy.”

“...Huh?”

For a moment, I could not comprehend what Naoe-sensei was saying, and for the first time gave such a stupid reply.

Kamogawa next to me must also be the same.

Among the quietness, an ear-scratching ringing sound was heard.

“Sensei, that is… Some kind of joke, right?”

The words came out of Kamogawa’s mouth, not as a confirmation, but as a reflex.

Even if he did not want to speak out of turn, I’m sure I would have said the same thing.

“It’s the truth. The candidates will be lined up tomorrow, and I will be voting for Kijima. I will also make you vote for him.”

Kijima? Why is Naoe-sensei supporting Kijima-sensei?

No matter how young and promising he is, Naoe-sensei’s position is higher.

“Please wait. Why? Naoe-sensei, what about all of your preparations for this time?”

Pitching forward, I could not push down my emotions no matter how much I tried.

I know that becoming the cabinet’s prime minister is not everything.

In fact, Naoe-sensei before me has been reigning for many years as a shadow fixer without a position, even though he has had many opportunities.

However, it is still the default route for him to become the Prime Minister now.

Rather, if he doesn’t run here… It is essentially the same thing as giving up the seat of the prime minister.

If he gives his seat up to Kijima-sensei once, that person will unmistakably keep his position.

Naoe-sensei’s faction will start losing its centripetal force, and he won’t have the opportunity to become prime minister again.

If he withdraws, one can’t help but think that something bad has happened.

Further, it will have a big effect on White Room.

It is precisely because I understood all this as an instinct, that I had to confirm.

The most surprising of all was the fact that the one Naoe-sensei decided to support was Kijima-sensei.

“K-Kijima sensei… He’s a clear enemy of yours, Naoe-sensei… Right..?”

Not able to hold back either, Kamogawa said the name out loud.

The candidates from the Citizens Party were narrowed down to three including those from inside and outside the government, and including the mass media.

The favourite being Naoe-sensei before me, the follower up being his rival Isomaru from the same period, and Kijima-sensei a bit further behind.

These three are the only ones who have a ticket to become the prime minister, and Naoe-sensei should have been the leading candidate.

“I didn’t want to make him the prime minister, but I ended up having to do just that.”

“Are you saying you could not gather the votes..?”

“That’s right. The votes inside the Citizens Party had been cleanly split between me, Isomaru, and Kijima, but, it looks like a part of the rival party has decided to crush me. The results ended up being that I lacked from 20 to 30 votes.”

I am sure he tried many strategies already, but I saw Naoe-sensei’s smile of resignation.

“Even if I play a good match, if I lose, I lose a lot of centripetal force. In which case, the only choice is to support Kijima and protect my position, isn’t it? He’s still young, and he has a lot of momentum. After thoroughly searching, he is not involved in any scandals either.”

Women, money, a politician with nothing to feel guilty of.

Further, he has also always been in good health.

“However, in that case, isn’t it better to support Isomaru-sensei? Even if you are rivals from the same period, you are acquaintances. I don’t think you need to support Kijima-sensei, who is difficult to handle, but…”

I’m sure he doesn’t have childish thoughts such as wanting to give the credit to someone from the same period.

If he judges that it is the correct thing to be under Kijima-sensei, then there will be no hesitation.

“You get it already, don’t you? The answer is that it is more beneficial to be under Kijima. If I force myself onto Isomaru’s ship now, the possibility of going down along with him is high. Between the two, most in my faction also want to support Kijima.”

There is the fear that even Naoe-sensei will be resisted if he tries to forcefully go over to Isomaru-sensei’s side.

I had no idea that he was driven into a corner like this.

I thought I understood everything about the political world, but it looks like even I didn’t know what was going on behind the scenes.

“It… It is too early to give up, Naoe-sensei. We still have the White Room project! If we use this as a weapon, we can-”

“Stop, Kamogawa.”

I strongly restrained Kamogawa who was trying to get to grips with this.

“If Naoe-sensei has made such a decision, then we must follow it. However, you understand that the White Room is a different issue, right?”

Supporting Kijima-sensei, Naoe-sensei will naturally be appointed again.

In other words, it should already be confirmed that his position will not change much.

I can say that this will not have that much of an impact.

However…

“Ayanokoji. That’s the matter I came to see you for today. After all these years of your hard work, I am sorry, but, I need you to keep quiet for a while.”

The last words I wanted to hear came flying out, and my cold sweats started flowing out.

“...What does that mean, Naoe-sensei?”

While beginning to understand the situation, I still could not accept it.

“I understand what you want to say. However, all of this will only happen if I can maintain my current position. You get it, right?”

“...Of course.”

“Sure, I've been unofficially promised my next post. However, it’s not something I've won. It was the last stronghold that I defended after losing the factional war. I can’t promote the White Room project, which has a high possibility of attracting controversy.”

If Naoe Sensei makes a bad move, Kijima-sensei’s side will not remain silent.

It is obvious that he would be suspected of trying to gain more influence by increasing his achievements again.

I understand that logic very well.

“Ayanokoji. You are an excellent man.”

“...Yes. Thank you very much.”

“After I picked up a have-not like you, you should know very well that I don’t solely judge people on the basis of academic record.”

“The world of politics has always required a certain academic record. If it was not for you, Naoe-sensei, no one would have employed a man like me.”

Naoe-sensei nodded, and sighed.

“For better or worse, people who have been involved in politics for a long time tend to follow their educational background and become incompetent. They care about nothing other than keeping their title as a politician. Both the politicians who want justice, and the ones who aim to be the bad guys get swallowed on the way all the same.”

Naoe-sensei was about to pick up the empty glass, but quickly pulled his hand away.

“Despite that, Kijima never changes. That fucking man is a real politician.”

I don’t think that Naoe-sensei has ever praised someone with words like this.

He is already doing the analysis of a war that has ended.

“I felt it with you too. Your direction is different, but you’re also a man that doesn’t change.”

“...Yes. Neither my conviction nor my ideals will ever change.”

“This country’s peak point… That was your aim, right?”

“Yes.”

“No hesitation, huh? However, that is to say that you want to defeat Kijima. He’s a considerable foe, you know?”

“I have the will. However, if you are saying that you will support Kijima-sensei, then please let me obey. From now on, for you, and for Kijima-sensei, I will-”

“I said it before too, but I need you to stay quiet for a while.”

Ah, I see.

I did have a bad feeling about this.

This means that I was right.

“...What do you mean?”

“You’ve become an eyesore for Kijima. It’s reached his ears many times that you’ve been involved in the business world in a gaudy way these few years. Do you understand? I can’t have someone like that under me.”

“I only want to do as you instruct me, Sensei. To create a facility that will surpass ANHS education, to change this country… Did you not tell me to be thorough?”

Naoe-sensei’s expression changed.

“Managing the White Room wasn’t fucking enough for you, so you’ve gathered up quite a lot of money too. You have connections with the yakuza too, and you’re not just a simple politician. Right? Isn’t that so? Did I tell you to do that much? You’ve been maneuvering for your own damn benefit. How many fires do you think I’ve put out these few years?”

His demeanour changed, and before I could notice, I was being strongly scolded.

“Then… What do you intend on doing about the White Room project?”

“Isn’t it obvious? A clean slate.”

“That’s… A clean slate..?”

Kamagawa, who had been explaining his joy up until just now, suddenly changed his expression to one of despair.

Stiff as a statue, I can’t deny that I had a stern expression.

A clean slate for the White Room project?

Does he know how hard I’ve worked up until now?

He can’t just sweep it all away with only the two words of “clean slate”.

…No, it was always this way.

With Naoe-sensei’s word, everything moves from right to left.

This isn’t anything special.

If I get clumsy and show resistance here, it will only make Naoe-sensei’s mood worse.

Even if only slightly, he must have come all the way here because he felt bad for us novices.

I have to be an adult here and react calmly, or I will lose my footing.

If I get dismissed as an impertinent man, there won’t be a second chance for me to be of use.

I gained enough money to gather the envy of others.

Even if I am abandoned by Naoe-sensei, I can probably keep on living.

However, as a politician… That would be it for me.

With that, my ambition would all come to nothing.

“That’s all. Don’t take it personally.”

Is it all over with this?

I don’t think Naoe-sensei intends on leisurely dining here.

That’s why he didn’t hold the glass once up until now.

“When Kijima accepts that you have no fangs, I’ll put you out in front again. You’re fine with that, right?”

To keep surviving as a politician.

I will throw away the White Room, and start again from scratch.

This is the best choice.

I know.

I know.

I know-

“...Please stop screwing around with me.”

This time, I couldn’t handle myself calmly and wisely as usual.

There’s no way I could.

How hard does he think I’ve worked for the sake of this project?

In these ten years, I gave everything up and grinded my body to dust.

I won’t let it all be for nothing.

“White Room is receiving a lot of funding and continuing to operate. There is no way I can withdraw now.”

“Huh? Who the fuck do you think you’re talking to, Ayanokoji?”

This incalculably overwhelming attitude before me, unfitting of an old man.

Without showing fear or timidity before my boldness, he simply looked at me with his pitch black pupils.

For Naoe-sensei who has been in the world of politics for decades, this kind of attitude is a daily occurrence.

Yet, I can’t pull back here now just because of that.

After pulling the bow, there is no more going back.

“I told you to return it to a clean slate. If you can’t do that, just fucking hang yourself and die.”

“Are you saying that now, after all this time?”

“Who gives a shit about your “after all this time?” ”

“I cannot accept this.”

“It doesn’t matter if you can accept it or not, I’m telling you that it’s over.”

“Then what will become of me? I looked up only to you, and threw away many opportunities for the sake of this project. Even if I can protect my title as a politician, it is pointless if I can’t do anything with it.”

“You’ll just have to endure for a few years. When that’s over, I’ll give you your next job.”

Can I believe him?

There’s no way I can.

“Under your instructions, I have been working only for this project. I cannot… I cannot accept such an illogical thing..!”

I have to lament.

I have no choice but to do so.

“I understand how you feel. But you should know how these things work. This is the kind of world we’re in. So don’t hate me, and keep backing me up. I was the one pushing you to help with the project’s progress, right? You were able to get elected with minimal work thanks to that. Isn’t that right?”

Indeed, I left all of the normal election processes up to Naoe-sensei.

And I am grateful to him for making me get elected.

“I am grateful for that. However-”

“Stop it, Ayanokoji. You’ll lose your footing if you get obsessed over one project.”

Why am I becoming so frantic?

I’m sure Kamogawa sitting silently beside me can’t even imagine why.

It’s not because I hate the idea of the White Room project ending and continuing to cling onto it. It’s because I understand the future.

This means that for Naoe-sensei, “I” have become something that needs to be used up and abandoned.

He will put me aside until the election time while telling me he will give me another chance, and when the time comes, he will cut me away without giving me any support.

Up until now, I’ve seen many politicians get cut off in the same manner right in front of my eyes.

In other words, when I was told the words “clean slate”, my destiny as a politician was decided.

In that case, my instincts that want to resist until the end chose to fight.

“In other words, you want me to be the only one covered in mud… right?”

“You’re still young. Unlike me, who doesn’t have much time left, you will have many other chances. But this is it for me. I can’t pull back here. I want to die as a politician, so.”

“Sensei…”

“I’m not telling you to quit being a politician. All you have to do is stay obedient.”

“Sensei, you will not abandon me or anything like that, right?”

“Of course. I won’t do anything bad to you. I spoke harshly, but, on the other hand, it seems like Kijima also values you highly. If you stay quiet, the bowl will come around. I can let you hold it when that time comes.”

It’s all over…

“I understand.”

“I see, good.”

“As you are telling me to, I will end the White Room project. I will start moving tomorrow to conduct the after processings.”

I deeply bowed.

“You do that.”

Naoe-sensei before my eyes no longer held even the slightest interest towards me.

It doesn’t matter if I am skilled or not. What matters is that he has to abandon me along with the project, and that he will no longer use me.

Part 1

“...Ugh.”

The only things in the room after Naoe-sensei left were Kamogawa crying and collapsing, and the cold food.

“What a fucking joke!”

I yelled out with anger.

“He’ll give the bowl to me later on? Don’t make me laugh…”

If you drop out from the world of politics once, it’s already over.

It doesn’t matter if I plan for a second coming, I will be crushed and ended.

“What are we going to do now..? Is it all over with this? Ah…”

Should I have beat him up..?

No, there’s no meaning in doing that and gaining momentary satisfaction.

I would be arrested right away, and lose not just my status as a politician, but everything else I’ve gained up until now.

It’s fine to show your strength in a physical fight if it’s a quarrel between brats.

However, in this world, physical strength is only one of the many weapons one can hold, and its effectiveness is weak.

Naoe, while looking like nothing more than a frail old man, holds an innumerable amount of weapons.

“Don’t think that you can use me up and get rid of me so easily, Naoe…”

I strongly hit the table with my fist, and spat out my anger.

Putting everything short, I was used up well and abandoned.

In the world of politics, there is no hope of getting back up if you fall down once.

“Am I finished?”

Even if put to words, I can’t feel any sense of reality towards this.

How much does he think I’ve anguished and sweated for the sake of moving this country, for the sake of rising to its summit?

Does he know how much I’ve been humiliated, insulted, and scorned?

That man is of no more use to me.

However, even if I try to fight back now in a new manner, I will simply be crushed and ended.

I can say that until that man retires, no, dies, the path of my success is completely closed off.

Then… This also means that if that man dies, I will have the chance to move again.

Do I contact Ooba, and have Naoe killed..?

“What a stupid idea…”

If I make such a request, it’ll only end up with Ooba abandoning me.

I don't even need to think about which option would be more beneficial.

“Kamogawa. Come to your senses, you’re going to start everything again from tomorrow.”

“That’s… That’s all I can do, but… Ayanokoji-sensei, what will you do? You will not ignore Naoe-sensei’s order, right?”

“...It’s all over for me, no matter what. My treatment won’t change now even if I stop resisting. In which case, I will continue managing the White Room even if I stop being a politician.”

“P-Please wait! I respect you so much, Ayanokoji-sensei! I think you’re the kind of man who will surpass Naoe-sensei one day! Please don’t say that you’ll retire!”

“My path is already set. I can’t change it any more. But, you can still save yourself. Your father’s influence still remains. So you should keep fighting as a politician under Naoe.”

“Ayanokoji-sensei…”

“I won’t give up politics, or White Room. But I will throw politics away for a moment.”

That is the one and only method.

“No matter how much power Naoe has, he can’t beat me in lifespan. One day, he will die before me.”

If it’s going to take time anyway, then I can just wait.

I will satisfy the old man and wait out his short political life.

However, when that’s over, I’ll…

“It’s not just Kijima. When I come back to the world of politics, I’ll burn his son to ashes too.”

“Haha… When you’re the one saying it, it doesn’t sound like a joke.”

Wiping away his tears and saying so, Kamogawa smiled.

Part 2

After putting Kamogawa in a taxi and letting him leave, I started walking alone on the snowy road.

I wanted to be alone, and cool down my hot head.

I have to think about the future.

That’s precisely why I need to know everything that is to come, and make the calculations in my head.

I made a call to that man with my phone.

It was late at night, but I was convinced that the call would certainly connect.

“Tsukishiro, answer me. Why did Naoe stick with Kijima, even throwing away our own positions?”

“This is going to be an interesting call, huh?”

“I’m sure you know everything. Right?”

“Up until now, Naoe-sensei thought that he was the best politician. However, he learned something, that Kijima is a presence even surpassing him.”

“How foolish.”

“The two have very different ideals, but they surprisingly also have a lot of common points.”

“With that… Am I supposed to be convinced with that?”

“Kijima-sensei is not happy about the White Room, which you’ve taken part in.”

“What are you saying? That man has ANHS education. He can even come out with it as his second policy.”

“That is certainly one of his main policies. However, he was also working on a similar new plan behind the scenes. In other words, his second policy was already in motion. It would not have been desirable for him to have it come out ahead of time.”

“...Is that the reason Naoe abandoned me..?”

“It isn’t clear at which stage it was, but word of White Room has reached Kijima-sensei’s ears. I think I can say that he talked with Naoe-sensei, and promised to let him keep his position afterwards if he brought it to a halt.”

So Kijima had also thought of a plan that resembled White Room, huh?

“It is not just that. You were much more capable than Naoe-sensei had even imagined. He relied on you a lot in these few years, but at the same time, did you not also think that he had a lot of unreasonable demands?”

“...Yeah.”

“I think that is because Sensei was afraid of you. It was as if he was waiting to crush you one day, rather than to make use of you. However, you did not fail. No, we cannot say that you did not have any failures at all. However, you conducted yourself well, erased any signs of failure and made sure you did not stand out. I have to admit that is quite the skill. Naoe-sensei did not raise you to put you on the top. I think that he was expecting you to support his son as his right hand when he rose up to the top of the country in the future. Sensei could see ahead in everything, but he made one miscalculation. Your limitless ambition, it seems that was the only thing he did not understand.”

After 10 years, even Naoe-sense won’t be able to crush me.

So, he played his hand now, as to prevent that from happening.

Do I consider it as a handout to his son, or a result of his fear that I would crush his son?

“Were you satisfied with the answer?”

“Why did you talk honestly?”

“If you were a man who would be crushed here, I would not have talked. However, my intuition tells me that this isn’t the case. One day, you will be back on the front stage. With even greater power. That is the reason I talked.”

“That is a wise judgement. Naturally, however, do you intend on showing up no matter how I fall?”

“That is the question.”

This man will not rely solely on me. He is already someone else’s ally, and it is the same for me.

If I am judged to be incapable, I am sure he will throw me away in that instant.

“You can sell my information to Naoe or anyone you want. In exchange, I will receive information from you. I am sure that a situation where we both can observe each other is preferable for both sides.”

“I am aware.”

“It seems we will associate with each other for a long time, Tsukishiro.”

“I hope so. If you have any requests, please contact me any time, Ayanokoji-sensei.”

Saying so, Tsukishiro hung up the phone.

That’s right, I won’t be finished here.

I will thoroughly prepare myself to protect my own life for the future, and gather my strength.

And at the same time, I will create my soldiers in White Room.

Part 3

200 meters tall, 50 floors.

The banquet floor in the middle of the ultra high-class hotel fighting for the top position in Tokyo.

Arriving slightly later than the planned time, I started going up in the elevator while thinking upon some things.

It costs about 3,000,000 to rent the floor for 3 hours and to serve meals for 60 people.

If I could say “only”, that would be about it, but considering my uncertain financial circumstances, it’s not cheap.

The party has been continuing every year since the operation of the facility started, and it’s been getting larger each time.

I have to collect a bigger amount of contributions than ever before.

Many investors turned their backs to me by Naoe’s hand.

It’s obvious from the fact that the 200 supporters I’ve had before decreased to 60.

Money, I need money. I have to scrape together manyfolds of money.

The necessary thing here today is to show my own strength.

I looked at my own eyes reflecting in the metal glass of the massive elevator.

I’ve gotten quite old too…

Looking back, I can calmly reflect on my own aging.

I think I can say that the fact that I’ve started and continued managing White Room is a miracle.

However, I am only half-way through.

It’s been some time since I was banished from the financial world, but the fires of my ambition, let alone being extinguished, are burning even hotter.

I reached my destination, got out of the elevator and headed to a vacant room.

Losing my title as a politician, I am now treated as a former politician.

Normally, my cohesive power would have reduced greatly.

However, the title of the man in charge of White Room is increasing my power.

If it wasn’t for that, the people I call investors would not have gathered in this place.

“Ayanokoji-sensei, it is about time.”

“Yeah.”

There is a lot to think about, but the most important problem to solve is the money.

As the scale gets larger, White Room requires more and more funding.

In order to provide this financing, I need more than an amount of money to use and throw away; I need money to keep living.

“S-sorry I kept you waiting.”

“You don’t seem calm, huh? Besides, how many times have you gone to the toilet now?”

Tabuchi, coming back to the vacant room, sat down on a chair in an uncalm demeanour, and started slightly shaking his left leg.

“When are you going to get rid of that habit?”

“I am sorry, but, when I think about the possibility of missing this chance… I feel uneasy.”

Indeed, if the funding is short, White Room will be in big trouble.

It will be alright if it just ends with a temporary freeze, but it is critical that the children’s education does not stop too.

It is the same as the baby birds you’ve raised getting sick and dying.

“Listen, Tabuchi. We can’t turn our backs to the fact that this is our last chance. However, that is precisely why we have to strongly step ahead without looking back. You can think about failure if we fail.”

The shaking of his left leg became slow, and Tabuchi looked up to me.

“Thinking about the difficulties we’ve gone through up until now, this isn’t anything special. Used up and abandoned by Naoe, ordered to bring White Room to a halt, and losing my title as politician.”

Still, I did not stop moving.

I can proudly say that I have led such a difficult life that I can’t even begin to tell other people about it.

Leaving aside people like Naoe and Kijima, it isn’t easy for even a mere politician to have an audience for me.

I did lose my title as a politician, but there is no mistaking the fact that I’ve surpassed my past self.

I noticed that Tabuchi had stopped shaking his leg, and was clenching his fist with strength.
