Prologue

Before I even realized it, I found myself all alone.

It’s not as if I am a particularly hated person.

I just never drew anyone’s attention.

I was always overshadowed and had no presence.

Leaving how nobody likes me aside, I couldn’t even manage to get anyone to dislike me. So I was always all by myself.

That’s how it was in kindergarten, primary school, and middle school too.

I never had any real friends and mostly spent my time alone.

I couldn’t develop the skills needed to talk to people, and just stayed invisible.

I thought that was okay, though. I kept trying to convince myself that this was the good thing about me…

I thought that it would be okay to keep living a lonely, quiet life after growing up too.

And yet… I certainly am here.

“...Yeah… I think losing doesn’t suit Sakayanagi-san…”

“Why don't you try telling her? Noone has any right to blame you for your actions.”

What is this feeling, this emotion seeping into my heart?

I don’t know.

I didn’t know.

I simply had no idea…

Until this day.
